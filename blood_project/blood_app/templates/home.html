About Our System

The Blood Bank Management System simplifies the process of blood donation and management. It connects donors and patients efficiently, ensuring that every drop counts. Donors can register and update their details, while patients can easily request the required blood type.

The system maintains real-time information about available blood groups, ensuring hospitals and patients get instant updates. It also helps reduce wastage and supports a well-organized healthcare workflow.

Available Modules

Admin Module

Admins can manage users, verify requests, and oversee operations.

Donor Module

Allows donors to register, log in, and record donation history.

Patient Module

Enables patients to request specific blood groups and track status.

Blood Stock Management

Manages availability of all blood groups in real time.

Authentication & Security

User login, signup, and secure data handling using Django authentication..

Blood Request Management

Handles patient requests and matches with available donors.

Blood Donation

Records blood donations, updates donor history and stock automatically.

Distribution Module

Handles issuance of blood to patients and updates request status and stock in real-time.
